This energy ball ,known as the Rasengan, is one of the most iconic and powerful jutsu techniques in the Naruto series. It was created by the Fourth Hokage of the Hidden Leaf Village, Minato Namikaze, and later perfected by his son, Naruto Uzumaki. The Rasengan is a pure chakra sphere created and controlled by the user's hand, and it doesn't require hand seals to perform, making it a highly advanced and versatile technique. When used in battle, the Rasengan causes devastating damage to opponents and is known for its capability to strike with immense force. Over the course of the series, Naruto enhances the Rasengan by adding different variations and elements to it, showcasing its adaptability and growth of his character development.
